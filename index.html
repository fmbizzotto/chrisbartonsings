About me

Chris is best known for his television appearances on hit BBC shows “Any Dream Will Do” & “All Together Now”, featuring in several episodes and being a finalist on both shows. Since appearing on television, Chris has also played a number of roles in London’s West End and on national tours.

Chris released an album, “Dream On” which made it to Number Two in the Easy Listening charts which then led him to headline a concert at the world famous London Palladium. Most recently, Chris has worked with many leading cruise lines, such as Royal Caribbean, P&O Cruises, Celebrity Cruises and Seabourn Cruises where his entertaining one-man show continues to wow audiences around the globe.
