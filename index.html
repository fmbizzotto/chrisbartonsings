Bio

Chris is best known for his television appearances on hit BBC shows “Any Dream Will Do” & “All Together Now”, featuring in several episodes and being a finalist on both shows. Since appearing on television, Chris has also played a number of roles in London’s West End and on national tours.

Chris released an album, “Dream On” which made it to Number Two in the Easy Listening charts which then led him to headline a concert at the world famous London Palladium. Most recently, Chris has worked with many leading cruise lines, such as Royal Caribbean, P&O Cruises, Celebrity Cruises and Seabourn Cruises where his entertaining one-man show continues to wow audiences around the globe.

Featured

The Sound of Music

Chris played the role of Rolf in Andrew Lloyd Webber’s lavish production of “The Sound of Music”, starring alongside Jason Donovan, Connie Fisher and Lesley Garrett.

Spring Awakening

Chris was cast as a Swing in the original London Cast of “Spring Awakening”, ultimately playing every male role in the production, including the leading role of Melchior Gabor. Chris was part of the cast from the very first previews at the Lyric Hammersmith Theatre and transferred with the company to the Novello Theatre for it's West End premiere.

Any Dream Will Do

Chris was a finalist in the hit BBC One TV show, where he made it to the live finals, singing every Saturday evening for the nation’s vote.

Hairspray

Chris played the role of Sketch in the original West End production of “Hairspray” at the Shaftesbury Theatre in London.

Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat

Chris joined the company of this much-loved show in 2007 where he played three different roles in the show: as Benjamin, as the Narrator and, in the title role of Joseph.
